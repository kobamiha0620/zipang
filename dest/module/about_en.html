"ZIPANG OPERA" is a music performance unit that delivers a completely new Japanese pop culture to the world, distinct from traditional Japanese culture.

As a new challenge for "ZIPANG OPERA", we will use a variety of musical approaches and the latest technology to deliver new Japanese pop to Japan and the world.

Together with the "ANCHORs" (fans of ZIPANG OPERA), we embark on a voyage on the "ZIPANG OPERA SHIP" towards the real and digital worlds, and an unknown world - "Another World" - as we set sail towards new horizons...

"ZIPANG OPERA" is a music performance unit consisting of four members: "Ryuji Sato", who has performed as Uchiha Sasuke in the live spectacle "Naruto", as "Yuji Itadori" in the stage play "Jujutsu Kaisen", and as Katsura Kogoro in the musical "Touken Ranbu", and has also held a solo live tour in Asia; "Yu Fukuzawa", who has appeared in "Attack on Titan - the Musical", Hyper Projection Play "Haikyuu", and "Black Butler: The Musical", and is also active as a choreographer; "spi", who has appeared in "Touken Ranbu", "Kuroko's Basketball", "Jersey Boys", "RENT", and other musicals, and is known for his overwhelming singing ability; and vocalist "Kokoronosuke", whose charming and memorable voice sets him apart in the world of traditional Japanese culture.

The name "ZIPANG OPERA" was chosen to reflect their performances, which are like operas for each song. They express a unique Japanese worldview through various musical approaches, such as EDM, electro-pop, mixtures, rap, and pop.

One of the highlights of their performances is how the worldview changes with each song, utilizing the strengths of each member. The artist-producer is the director and active performer "PaniCrew", "Tsuyoshi Ueki". They aim to share this new style of Japanese pop music with the world.
